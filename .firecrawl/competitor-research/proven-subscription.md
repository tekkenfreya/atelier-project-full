## FAQs

#### Can I use other skincare products with my System?

Yes, but we recommend simplifying your routine. Your personalized formulas work best when used together as a complete system.

#### How long will my products last?

Each set is designed to last about 6–8 weeks with consistent use.

#### When will I see results?

As part of our PROVEN Promise, 90% of our customers begin to see noticeable results within 90 days of consistent use, as the skin naturally renews itself through several cell turnover cycles. It's common to start noticing subtle improvements within the first 2 to 3 weeks of consistent use, such as increased hydration, smoother texture, and suppleness. However, more significant changes may take several weeks to months to become apparent as your skin consistently renews with the use of your PROVEN System.

#### What if my skin reacts or changes?

Contact our skincare experts, and we’ll adjust your formula at no extra cost within 30 days

#### How is my 3-step System personalized?

Your 3-Step System is powered by PROVEN’s Skin Health Index™—our revolutionary skincare database built from billions of validated data points, including peer-reviewed research, clinical studies, and real-world insights. Our SkintellectAI™ technology then transforms those insights into a complete, personalized routine—Cleanser, Day Moisturizer, and Night Cream—designed to work in harmony with your unique skin profile, environment, and evolving needs.
