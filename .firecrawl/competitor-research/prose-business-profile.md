# Prose

Custom haircare.

## About

### Founded

2017

### Headquarters

New York, New York

### Industry

Haircare, DTC

### Company Description

At Prose, we know one size fits none. So we re-thought the entire model of mass beauty to create one-of-a-kind haircare and skincare. It’s a radically custom approach: We create products that fit you (and only you), combining lab innovation, natural ingredients, and patent-pending AI — for truly custom care and always-improving results.

## Company Traction

- As of June 2023, Prose has 3.4 million+ customer profiles, 400K+ 5-star reviews, 84% of customers would recommend to a friend, and examine 85+ factors to create their customer formulas for its products.

- As of June 2023, Prose has 288K Instagram followers and 50K Facebook followers.

- As of August 2022, Prose has [250,000](https://www.glossy.co/beauty/approaching-100-million-in-sales-prose-goes-after-curly-haired-customers/) active subscribers and is on pace for $100 million in sales in 2022.

- As of November 2018, Prose has 135 data points on [~500,000](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/prose-raises-18-million-series-b-funding-to-expand-custom-hair-care-300755151.html) customer profiles.

- As of November 2018, Prose’s proprietary manufacturing system can customize [10,000](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/prose-raises-18-million-series-b-funding-to-expand-custom-hair-care-300755151.html) products daily.
