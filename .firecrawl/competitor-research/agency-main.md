## Questions?  _We’ve got answers._

01What is the custom Future Formula?

Your Future Formula is a custom cream made for your unique skin by your dermatology provider. It will be designed for your needs and goals to target skin concerns like fine lines, texture, firmness, and more. Your dermatology provider will review your answers and photos, and prescribe custom ingredients—at a custom strength—for your skin’s unique needs. Before each new shipment, we’ll check in to see how you’re doing—and if needed, we’ll adjust your prescription to ensure it is customized for your changing skin.

* * *

02What is the Dark Spot Formula?

The Dark Spot Formula is a personalized dark spot corrector designed to work alongside your multitasking Future Formula. Help fade areas of darkened skin such as melasma, sunspots and other types of hyperpigmentation with prescription ingredients selected for your unique skin. Your dermatology provider will select custom ingredients, at custom strengths, to include in your Dark Spot Formula.

* * *

03What are the key ingredients in the Future Formula?

Your active ingredients–and their strength level—will be selected for your skin and goals by your dermatology provider. They may include: tretinoin, azelaic acid, tranexamic acid, niacinamide, and more. In addition to active ingredients, your formula will also include inactive, or base, ingredients that vary depending on your individual formula. See the full list of possible base ingredients [here](https://support.withagency.com/en_us/ingredients-BJIaQh_2O).

All Agency formulas are vegan, cruelty-free, unscented, and paraben-free.

* * *

04Who are the Agency dermatology providers?

Our team of dermatology medical providers are experts in fine lines, dark spots, skin texture, and more. We’ll match you with one who is licensed in your state—and will become an expert on your unique skin. They’ll create your custom routine, answer questions, and make adjustments as needed. So don’t be a stranger. You can always message your dermatology provider through your Agency account if you have questions. We’re here for you.
