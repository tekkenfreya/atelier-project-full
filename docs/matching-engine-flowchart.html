Step A: What does the customer need?
Read Q12 answers. Customer selected: "Breakouts or blemishes", "Oiliness or excess shine"
Step B: What does each product treat? (Layer 2)
The engine needs to know what each product is good for. It checks two sources:

Source 1 — Concern tags (if filled in by Kyrill in the ERP):
Product is tagged with "Breakouts or blemishes" in the ERP → direct match. No translation needed.

Source 2 — Ingredient lookup (fallback if no concern tags):
Product has Salicylic Acid → Layer 2 ingredient map says "Salicylic Acid = breakouts" → match.
This is a hardcoded reference table in the code — not AI. It maps ingredient names to what they treat.
